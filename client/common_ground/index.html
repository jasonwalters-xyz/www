Common Ground

Common Ground is a multi-layered immersive journey into the history, politics, and human face of the current crisis in the UK housing system. Content is a mix of stereoscopic 360 film, archive video, photogrammetry, soundscapes, and 3D modelling. As one of the primary developers I brought the creative team's vision to life by designing and implementing the experience in Unity.
