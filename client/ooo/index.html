OOO Experience

Created for Cartoon Network, the OOO Experience was an interactive installation using the Kinect for full-body tracking of up to 4 players. Participants could battle alongside and become Adventure Time stars Finn, Jake, Princess Bubblegum, and Marceline to save the Candy Kingdom. The experience toured around the UK and Europe in 2016. As lead developer I created the experience in Unity and worked closely with the creative team.
