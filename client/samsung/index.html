Samsung Fan

This project elevated the 2019 Dallas Cowboys NFL season by delivering hyper-accurate, persistent AR experiences that brought fans closer to the action than ever before. From any seat in the stadium, fans could engage with photo-realistic holograms, interactive games, and live stats in real-time. I was lead developer on the Time Tackle game and provided support across other areas of the project.
