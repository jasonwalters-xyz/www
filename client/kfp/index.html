Kung Fu Panda 4

Meet the one and only Kung Fu Panda in a unique Mixed Reality encounter where the Dragon Warrior himself takes time out of his training to teach you some meditative moves. Follow Po's lead using your whole body to copy his moves, and achieve a moment of tranquility atop the misty mountains in the Valley of Peace. As lead developer I was in charge of designing and implementing the architecture, app features, and wiring up studio assets while collaborating closely with creative, development, and studio teams.
