Alphonse Mucha

A touring VR experience of Alphonse Mucha's 'The Slavs in Their Original Homeland' (1912), part of 'The Slav Epic' — a large-scale 20-canvas series chronicling the history of Czechs and Slavic people. As lead developer I collaborated closely with the creative team to bring the experience to life.
