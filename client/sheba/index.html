Sheba Hope Reef

Launched on World Ocean Day, this narrative-led AR activation celebrates SHEBA's work on the world's largest coral restoration program, allowing people to explore the Sheba Hope Reef off the coast of Indonesia from anywhere in the world. As lead developer I was responsible for creating the experience in Unity and worked closely with the creative and technical directors.
