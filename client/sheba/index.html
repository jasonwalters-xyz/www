Details

Launched on World Ocean Day, the narrative-led AR activation celebrates SHEBA’s work on the world’s largest coral restoration program, allowing people to explore the Sheba Hope Reef, situated off the coast of Indonesia, from anywhere in the world.

As the lead developer, I was responsible for creating the experience in Unity and worked closely with both the creative director and technical director.
