Details

Bruto is a minimalist puzzle game about color and consequence.

Each tap triggers a chain reaction as dots shift between white, grey, and red. Every move affects the board. The goal is simple: leave no red dots behind.

Designed for focused, unhurried play, Bruto removes timers and distractions. Just deliberate interaction, subtle haptics, and carefully constructed puzzles that reward planning and restraint.

Featuring 50 handcrafted levels, Bruto offers a quiet, thoughtful puzzle experience you can play entirely offline.

Features
* Minimalist visual design
* 50 handcrafted puzzles
* Chain-reaction gameplay built around color
* Subtle haptic feedback
* Game Center achievements
* Fully offline play
* No ads or interruptions

How to Play
* Swipe left — next level
* Swipe right — previous level
* Swipe up — reset puzzle
* Swipe down — level select
* Clear all red dots in 10 moves or fewer to fully complete a level
