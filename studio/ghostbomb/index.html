Ghostbomb

Ghostbomb is an interactive art experience that allows users to create a ripple effect with a single touch. The impact of multiple touches is interdependent and produces unique outcomes. By altering the direction of movement, particles can be manipulated to either attract or repel, but return to their original position upon release.
