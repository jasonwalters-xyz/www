Eyka

Eyka is a quiet puzzle experience centered on color, form, and focus. Each puzzle is explored by rotating a structure in space and tapping blocks to shift color. Every interaction affects its neighbors, creating evolving relationships that reward observation and intention. Across six carefully composed color chapters, the visual tone subtly changes as you progress. No timers, no distractions — just thoughtful interaction and a sense of balance unfolding over time.
