Details

Eyka is a quiet puzzle experience centered on color, form, and focus.

Each puzzle is explored by rotating the structure in space and tapping blocks to shift color. Every interaction affects its neighbors, creating evolving relationships that reward observation and intention.

Across six carefully composed color chapters, the visual tone subtly changes as you progress, maintaining clarity while introducing variation and depth.

Eyka is designed to feel calm, intentional, and intuitive. There are no timers, no distractions, and no pressure — just thoughtful interaction and a sense of balance unfolding over time.

Designed For
- Focus and calm
- Minimal interaction
- Unhurried play

Features
- Intuitive, minimalist puzzle design
- Six curated color chapters
- No ads, no timers, no interruptions
- Fully offline play
- 108 handcrafted puzzles
